By enabling the Development portfolio, a new 'Portfolio' tab becomes availabe. There you see an overview of all your ongoing projects. You can assign a rank to your projects, to represent the the relative importance of your ongoing projects. While it currently has no effect elsewhere in Agilefant, you can use it to record your thoughts on strategic decisions.

On this tab you can also create custom views to view selected Stories, Iterations, Projects and User workloads.
